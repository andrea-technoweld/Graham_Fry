Work History

Career Positions

1996 — Present Director / Principal

Technoweld Pty. Ltd.

Welding Management Consulting & Inspection

Founded in 1996, Technoweld has delivered welding management consulting, inspection, procedure development, training, and quality system implementation to clients across Australia and internationally. Work spans pressure vessels, high-pressure piping, structural steel, oil and gas, mining, chemical, infrastructure, and food processing sectors.

2010 — Present Founding Director

Australian Welding Institute Ltd.

National Welding Industry Body

Founding Director of the AWI, serving on the Federal Board of Directors for over a decade. Roles have included Technical Director, Director of Marketing, and Chair of the Education Certification Committee — helping shape the direction of welding certification and standards across Australia.

1996 — 2007 Lecturer

TAFESA

Engineering Faculty — Education Provider

Over a decade as a lecturer in the Engineering Faculty, teaching welding supervision, inspection, senior fabrication inspection, and welder certification. Training remains one of Graham's genuine passions — he has seen first-hand what happens when tradespeople don't receive the right support and education.

1988 — 1996 Tradesperson

Pipetech Pty. Ltd.

Manufacturer of Pressure Equipment & Associated Structures

Where it all began. Eight years fabricating pressure equipment and associated structures for the oil, gas, chemical, and food industries. The hands-on foundation built here — the hard-won knowledge of what works and what doesn't when metal meets heat — informs every decision Graham makes today.
