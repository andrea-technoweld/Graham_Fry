How it works

Practical Help, Without the Corporate Wrapper

When you engage me, you get me. Not an account manager fronting the meetings while a junior engineer does the actual work. An International Welding Engineer with nearly 40 years in the industry, on your problem directly.

I work with project teams, fabrication managers, and engineering leads to understand the actual problem, not just the symptom. Most engagements start with a conversation. I'll ask blunt questions, give you a straight assessment, and tell you what I think it'll take to fix.

The scope is whatever the job needs. That might be a single welding procedure qualification, a pre-audit gap analysis against ISO 3834 or AS/NZS 5131, or a one-day site review. Equally, I've supported companies through multi-year quality system implementations, supplier quality programmes, and ongoing embedded welding engineering roles.

I provide welding consulting across Australia and internationally. Remote advisory work covers many services, including procedure reviews, specification development, and documentation audits. Site visits are arranged where the work needs eyes on the job, and sometimes a helmet on.

Services

What I Can Help You With

Practical welding engineering services delivered by someone who has spent a career doing the work, not just advising on it.

Welding Optimisation & Productivity

Practical process improvement on the shop floor, lifting throughput while driving rework down.

  • Welding process and parameter reviews (GMAW, FCAW, SAW, GTAW)
  • Consumable and process selection for productivity gains
  • Weld sequencing and distortion control
  • Rework and defect rate reduction
  • First-time-pass quality improvement
  • Measured against real job data, not theory

Design for Manufacture

Reviewing drawings before steel gets cut, driving down cost and rework through smarter, practical weld detailing.

  • Drawing and weld detail reviews at design stage
  • Right-sizing welds: where full penetration isn't required
  • Joint configurations that suit the welder, the position, and the access
  • Weld symbol accuracy to AS 1101.3 and AWS A2.4
  • Distortion and tolerance planning before fabrication starts
  • Specification reviews that cut over-welding without cutting compliance

Quality Systems, Audits & Certification Support

ISO 3834 system development paired with third-party audit and inspection capability — under one engagement.

  • ISO 3834 Parts 2, 3, and 4 gap analysis and system development
  • Quality system documentation and internal audit preparation
  • Certification body liaison and pre-audit readiness
  • Fabrication shop capability assessments and supplier evaluations
  • In-process welding inspections and AS/NZS 5131 Construction Category assessments
  • NATA-associated inspection activities

Expert Witness & Disputes

Technical expert services for welding-related disputes, insurance claims, and litigation.

  • Weld failure investigation and root cause analysis
  • Technical reports for legal proceedings
  • Expert evidence — written and oral
  • Independent assessment of disputed weld quality
  • Mediation support

Training & Competency Development

Practical, industry-focused training for welding supervisors, inspectors, and engineers.

  • Welding Supervisor competency (ISO 14731)
  • WPS/PQR interpretation and management
  • AS/NZS 1554 and AS 4458 practical application
  • ISO 3834 awareness and implementation
  • Customised workshops for project teams

Project & Embedded Support

Dedicated welding engineering resources for projects needing technical oversight.

  • Project welding engineering plans
  • Pre-construction welding reviews
  • Welder qualification management
  • NCR investigation and disposition
  • Site and workshop technical support
