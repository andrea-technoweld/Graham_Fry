How it works

Practical Help, Without the Corporate Wrapper

When you engage me, you get me — not an account manager and a junior engineer doing the actual work.

I work directly with project teams, fabrication managers, and engineering leads to understand the actual problem — not just the symptom. Most engagements start with a conversation. I'll ask blunt questions, give you a straight assessment, and tell you what I think it'll take to fix.

I'm happy working on a short-scope basis — a single procedure qualification, a pre-audit gap analysis, or a one-day site review. Equally, I've supported companies through multi-year quality system implementations and ongoing embedded engineering roles. The scope is whatever the project actually needs.

I operate across Australia and internationally. Remote advisory work is possible for many services — site visits are arranged where the work requires physical presence.

Services

What I Can Help You With

Practical welding engineering services delivered by someone who has spent a career doing the work, not just advising on it.

📐

Welding Procedure Development

Development and qualification of WPS, PQR, and WQT to Australian and international standards.

  • AS/NZS 1554 Series (structural, stainless, pipelines)
  • AS 4458 / AS 3992 (pressure equipment)
  • ASME IX, ASME VIII
  • ISO 15614, ISO 15613
  • API 1104, AWS D1.1
  • Pre-qualified and fully qualified joints
🏆

ISO 3834 Quality Certification

Gap analysis, system development, and pre-audit preparation for ISO 3834 Parts 2, 3, and 4.

  • Gap analysis against your chosen conformance level
  • Quality system documentation development
  • Internal audit preparation
  • Certification body liaison support
  • Ongoing compliance maintenance
🔍

Independent Audits & Inspections

Third-party welding quality audits for fabricators, contractors, and project owners.

  • Fabrication shop capability assessments
  • In-process welding inspections
  • Pre-contract supplier evaluations
  • AS/NZS 5131 Construction Category assessments
  • NATA-associated inspection activities
⚖️

Expert Witness & Disputes

Technical expert services for welding-related disputes, insurance claims, and litigation.

  • Weld failure investigation and root cause analysis
  • Technical reports for legal proceedings
  • Expert evidence — written and oral
  • Independent assessment of disputed weld quality
  • Mediation support
🎓

Training & Competency Development

Practical, industry-focused training for welding supervisors, inspectors, and engineers.

  • Welding Supervisor competency (ISO 14731)
  • WPS/PQR interpretation and management
  • AS/NZS 1554 and AS 4458 practical application
  • ISO 3834 awareness and implementation
  • Customised workshops for project teams
🏗

Project & Embedded Support

Dedicated welding engineering resources for projects needing technical oversight.

  • Project welding engineering plans
  • Pre-construction welding reviews
  • Welder qualification management
  • NCR investigation and disposition
  • Site and workshop technical support
